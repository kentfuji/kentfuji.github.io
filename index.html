About Me

Kent Fujiwara is a research scientist at LINE Corporation. His research interests is in computer vision, mainly dealing with point cloud analysis, 3D vision, image registration and 3D reconstruction. He is the lead scientist at Computer Vision Lab in AI Company at LINE Corporation.
