Kent Fujiwara


I am a research scientist at LY Corporation. My research interests is in computer vision, mainly dealing with point cloud analysis, 3D vision, image registration and 3D reconstruction. Along with my colleagues I lead a lab called Virtual Human Laboratory between LY Corporation and Osaka University.

News: We have one paper accepted to ECCV 2024! Stay tuned for more details.
